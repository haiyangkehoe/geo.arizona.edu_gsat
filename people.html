People

The Global Seismology and Tectonics Group at the University of Arizona consists of three faculty members, Susan Beck, Eric Kiser, and George Zandt, and an associated research group that normally consists of 1 post-doc and 4-8 graduate students, as well as undergraduate students and collaborating students from other groups in the Department of Geosciences.
