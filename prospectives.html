Prospective Student Information

Students interested in Global Seismology should contact Susan Beck and Eric Kiser, and apply to the graduate program at the Department of Geosciences. Applications are due in January for Fall admission. More information about the graduate program can be found at the Prospective Students Section of the Department of Geosciences website.
